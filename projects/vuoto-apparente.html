Visual identity

Vuoto Apparente
Art Collective

2021

Identity for Vuoto Apparente (Apparent Void), a public art exhibition that took place in Milan. Twelve artworks have been spread across the city theater and cinema’s bulletin boards, left empty by the pandemic. The identity revolves around the presence and its contrary; by overlapping the exhibition’s initials we get a broken X, a symbol used to signal something, as well as the absence, of something. The color used is process yellow, a warning color through which cities communicate with the citizens.
