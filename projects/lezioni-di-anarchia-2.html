Editorial design for

Lezioni di Anarchia

(

Anarchy lessons

), a project born from public talks on anarchist and libertarian thinking, organized yearly by Edicola 518 and publisher elèuthera editrice. What is the anarchic position about topics such as politics, family, education and work? Intellectuals from all over the country came to Perugia to tell anarchism as it really is: a way of conceiving life with others without reciprocal domain. The transcriptions of the meetings with insights into historical events, names and works eventually found their place onto printed paper. The result is a rich hypertext, a notebook in which new ways of imagining and actualizing life with others are outlined. The book, in its layout and binding solutions, is designed to prompt the reader to interact with the volume by taking notes and extracting pages, fostering a personal learning journey.

Curated by Emergenze Publishing and illustrated by Beppe Giacobbe.
