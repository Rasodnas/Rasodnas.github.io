Editorial design

Emergenze Publishing
Paolo Ventura

2022

Design of a poetry book series for Emergenze Publishing. The books collect the unread pen of amateur yet insightful authors. Each book has its own silk–printed typography, coloured paper, cover artwork and inner layout scheme designed to match the metric and atmosphere of each autor’s poetry. The cover artworks are made by visual artist and photographer Paolo Ventura.
