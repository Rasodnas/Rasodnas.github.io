Book design for Teatro Stabile dell'Umbria, with Emergenze Publishing. This book collects dramaturg Linda Dalisi’s efforts in preparing the groundwork for Antonio Latella's rewriting of Who's Afraid of Virginia Woolf. The author explains what it means to be a researcher in the theater field, documents the collective process of bringing a play on stage and writes an in-depth biography of american playwright Edward Albee. The book layout is inspired by dramaturg’s main instruments: notebooks, post-its and conceptual maps. It alternates essays to diaristic content.
