3D Animation and Modeling

3D Animation – Politecnico di Milano
with Valentina Zaccaria

2020

Animation and basic modeling for the 3D Animation course at Politecnico di Milano. All elements were modeled, textured, rigged and animated from scratch by Raffaello Chiarioni and Valentina Zaccaria. The run and jump animations were first handrawn and then re-created by keyframing the main poses and manually adjusting the in-betweening.
