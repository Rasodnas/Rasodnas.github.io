Identity, editorial design and illustrations made for

Teatro Sant'Ercolano

, a series of artistic performances organized by Edicola 518 in Perugia. The cultural initiative aimed to regain contact with public spaces after the city's lockdown in 2021, and did so by choosing the long time inactive Neptune fountain to be the stage of a season of artistic events. A bulletin that documented the artistic endeavour was published after every event. The logo represents the sprouting fountain stylized in the shapes of a T with two archs above it (symbols of the city district) and its drawn differently for every performance. The season ends with the Neptune fountain being reactivated.
