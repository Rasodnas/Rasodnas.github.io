Identity, editorial design and illustrations made for

Teatro Sant'Ercolano

, a series of artistic performances organized by Edicola 518 in Perugia. The cultural initiative aimed to claim public spaces back after the city's 2021 severe lockdown and did so by declaring the long time inactive Neptune fountain, at the center of Sant’Ercolano district, to be the stage of a series of artistic happenings. Eventually, six art performances took place in the city district, each time accompanied by a bulletin that documented them. The logo represents the sprouting fountain, stylized in the shapes of a T with two archs above it (symbols of the city district) and its drawn differently for every performance. The season ends with the Neptune fountain being reactivated.
