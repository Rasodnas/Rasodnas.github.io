Editorial design


Marco Baldicchi

2017

Catalogue design for

Acqua Pura Poesia

, a performance made by conceptual artist Marco Baldicchi. The action consisted in writing, with a water wet brush, verses from Leopardi's poem

A Silvia

directly on the city pavement. The words dried few moments after they were written, forcing the artist in a perpetuous ritual that didn’t end until the water bucket was empty.
