Visual identity and catalogue design for

Lo spazio che occupo

(

The Space I Occupy

) an exhibition that took place during PerSo Film Festival – 2020 reflecting on space as a physical and social dimension. Twelve artists were asked to create a visual artwork that would be placed in Perugia’s public billboards. The visual identity lies in the idea of filling up space by pushing the four words of the exhibition name towards the edges of its container. This typography system was concieved to both frame the artworks and to act as an iconic identity language. The catalogue was printed in 26x39cm large format.
