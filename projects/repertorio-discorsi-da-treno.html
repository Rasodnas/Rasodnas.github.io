Editorial Design Course

2021

Editorial design project for my Bachelor degree at Politecnico di Milano. In the final course, held by prof. Mario Piazza, students were given a book and asked to reinterpret its form, by maintaining the text printed in the original edition. I worked with Giovanni Randaccio’s

Repertorio dei discorsi da treno

(

Train-Talk Repertoire

), where the author noted conversations heard in public trains. I decided to enhance the original idea by building a sort of database, through which the discourses could be sorted by topic, place, tone of voice, number of actors involved, etc. A set of icons were made to represent the conversations and to guide the reader in the consultation of the repertoire. The book form consists in a collection of single punctured pages that could be read and re-arranged in the preferred order.
