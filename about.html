About

Welcome to this repository, I'm Raffaello, an italian freelance graphic designer. I design visual identities, books, catalogues, posters, ads, illustrations, animations, websites, user interfaces, presentations, infographics and whatever you may need that requires information to be visually managed, or a message to be meaningfully delivered.

Work

I work with both private businesses and public institutions, with a focus on art and culture. Some recent collaborations include Fondazione Burri, Tipografia Grifani Donati 1799, Santa Maria della Scala di Siena, Galleria dell'Accademia di Firenze, Istituto dei Ciechi di Milano, PerSo Film Festival, Teatro Stabile dell'Umbria, Emergenze Publishing, elèuthera editrice, Edicola 518.
