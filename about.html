About

Welcome to this repository, My name is Raffaello, and I am an italian freelance graphic designer. I design visual identities, books, catalogues, posters, ads, illustrations, animations, websites, user interfaces, presentations, infographics and whatever you may need that requires information to be visually managed, or a message to be efficiently delivered.

CV

Raffaello Chiarioni was born 1996 in Città di Castello, Italy. He studied Visual Communication at Politecnico di Milano and graduated in 2020 under the supervision of prof. Mario Piazza. Since 2019, he has been working closely with italian artistic collective Emergenze / Edicola 518 and its publishing house Emergenze Publishing, designing the majority of their publications and artistic endeavours. He officially began his freelance practice in 2021, collaborating with private companies, cultural institutions and communication agencies. Recent collaborations and clients include:

Gummy Industries, Digital design agency– Graphic Designer (2024–2025)
