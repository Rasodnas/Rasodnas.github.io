ABOUT ME

I began my freelance career while attending the first classes at Politecnico di Milano, where I've graduated in Visual Communication Design in 2020.

I work with artists, public institutions and private companies, in both commercial and artistic endeavours.

I have collaborated for more than three years with Edicola 518, giving visual form to the Italian art collective's main editorial and artistic projects.

ABOUT THIS SITE

I consider this website both as a tool to forge future collaborations, as well as a mirror that helps me to keep track of where I am leading my practice, and where she wants to lead me next.

This handcrafted grid-block website, is concieved not only to host commissioned projects, but also self initiated work and items that are meaningful for constituting my personal universe of meaning, that eventually will be put in appropriate ITEM BAGS.

First, I am currently still uploading all the projects that I showed in the home page (plus more), so it could take a while to see a lively populated database, as this site is intended to be.
