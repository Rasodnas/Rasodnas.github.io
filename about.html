About my work

Welcome to this site, I'm Raffaello, an italian freelance graphic designer. I design visual identities, books, catalogues, posters, ads, illustrations, animations, websites, user interfaces, presentations, infographics and whatever you may need that requires information to be visually managed, or a message to be meaningfully delivered.

Education

ITIS Franchetti Salviani – Information Technologies (High School Diploma, 2015)
Nuovo Istituto Design di Perugia – Graphic Design (Graphic Designer Diploma, 2016)
Politecnico di Milano – Communication Design (Bachelor degree, 2020)
Clients

I work for both fields of art and business. I started my freelance career during university, by working for companies, cultural institutions, museums and art collectives such as Munich Re, Fondazione Burri, Tipografia Grifani Donati 1799, Santa Maria della Scala di Siena, Galleria dell'Accademia di Firenze, Istituto dei Ciechi di Milano, PerSo Film Festival, Teatro Stabile dell'Umbria, Emergenze Publishing, elèuthera editrice and Edicola 518.
Exhibitions

In 2023,

Lezioni di Anarchia

, an editorial project made with Emergenze Publishing and elèuthera editrice, was selected for

ITALY: A New Collective Landscape

, an exhibition made by ADI Design Museum – Milano which aims to highlight the state of today’s young italian design scene by collecting projects born from collaborative practices.
About this site

This handcrafted repository is both a place for you to consult my work, as well as a place for me to keep track of what I've done and reason on what I'd like to do next. The site is still updating but, while I'm working on uploading more images and projects, the first dozen should be enough for you to understand my visual and conceptual approach to the discipline.
